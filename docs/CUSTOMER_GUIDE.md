## Account Access

## Account Access

- You will receive a login or SSO link from your provider.
- If SSO is enabled (e.g., WHMCS/Paymenter), use your provider credentials.

## Service Overview

- Your service includes a control panel, console access, file management, and backups (if enabled by the provider).
- Resource limits (CPU/RAM/disk) are enforced per your plan.

## Common Tasks

- **Start/stop servers**: Use the server controls in the panel.
- **Upload files**: Use the File Manager; large files may be restricted.
- **Restore backups**: Available if backups are enabled for your plan.

## Support & SLA

- If your server is offline or suspended, contact your provider’s support.
- Include server name/ID and timestamps in tickets.

## Data & Retention

- Backups and logs may have retention policies configured by your provider.
- Ask your provider for retention periods and restore procedures.

## Security Best Practices

- Use strong passwords and enable MFA if available.
- Share access only with trusted collaborators.
- Rotate tokens/credentials if exposure is suspected.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change networking mode?**
A: Only if your plan/role permits it. Ask your provider.

**Q: Why can’t I access certain features?**
A: Roles and permissions restrict access; contact your provider.
